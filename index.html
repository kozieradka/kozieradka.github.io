About me

I like to make products with silky smooth interfaces that people will enjoy using again and again. I firmly believe that good design is all about asking the right questions. Starting a dialogue allows me to recognise people's needs and put myself in their shoes. That's why I question everything, especially my own ideas.

Despite being comfortable with making my own design decisions I believe that the creative process and solving problems collaboratively is much more effective. I work hard on gaining people’s trust and finding the best way to communicate with every person in the team individually.

I began my career as a graphic designer and pen and paper are my favourite tools to this day. Even when I am not responsible for the visual part of a project I never underestimate the value of good typography and graphical interface. I am always trying to find the balance between usefulness and attraction.
